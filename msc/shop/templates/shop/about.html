HandMart

Hand Mart Private Limited is an Indian Multinational e-commerce company, headquartered in Bangalore, and incorporated in Singapore as a private limited company. The company initially focused on groceries before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. . As of FY23, Hand Mart held a 48% market share in the Indian e-commerce industry. We focus on empowering every Indian by delivering value through innovation and technology. In an attempt to make high-end product accessible to all, our No Cost EMI plan enable you to shop under EMI without any processing fee.
